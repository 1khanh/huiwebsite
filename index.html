About Us

Hawaii Undergraduate Initiative (HUI) is a summer transitional program that provides a free 3-credit college course, textbooks, and bus pass for incoming freshmen at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. In addition to providing a free college course, HUI offers engaging and insightful resource workshops, exploratory workshops, as well as writing and math workshops. Each HUI scholar will be paired with a Peer Mentor who will support and assist them in their transition from high school to college.
